The Vijaydurg Fort | Navel Strength of the Konkan

History
The fort of Vijaydurg is situated in the Devgad Taluka of Sindhudurg District that is well known for Alphonso mangoes. Vijaydurg Fort is around 400 metres away from Vijaydurg Port which is a minor port and one of the significant harbours on the western coast. It even includes the 40 KM long Waghotan/Kharepatan creek. The fort was even being referred to as 'Gheria ' as it is situated in the town 'Girye'. Vijaydurg being a sea fort seems to be harmed a ton by the tides and waves. However, during the time of Shivaji Maharaj, the triple line of fort walls, the various towers, and the gigantic inside structures were constructed. This fort is additionally known for naval fights fought for centuries to get control over the fort. We can see several remains of medieval structures on the fort added to it over the period. The underwater structural wall near the fort to protect it makes this a unique coastal fort.
